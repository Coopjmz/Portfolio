About Me

I'm a Game Developer

The most important part in game development is inspiration. I got inspired by games like Super Mario, Counter Strike 1.6, Mortal Kombat, World of Warcraft etc. when I was still a little kid and thus my gaming life began. I've been playing games ever since and never got tired of it. At the age of 14 I started going into computer science and coding became one of my primary activities. Not so long afterwards... I wanted to make games myself! :)

I started learning all programing languages which are necessary for a game developer: C++, C#, Lua and Python (for scripting) and even Java because I wanted to make a Minecraft mode. After building some games from scratch (a.k.a. creating your own game engine with different APIs like SDL and SFML), I decided to give game engines a try and Unity was the first in my list. I was really surprised at how simple everything was, compared to builing it on your own. And thus I created my latest game - Sunnyland.

My Work

The project I've been working on

Sunnyland

Sunnyland is a pixel art 2D platformer adventure game, where you play as a fox and collect cherries while fighting vicious enemies like... frogs. But don't be mistaken, you are not just an ordinary fox! You are actually a father and your little child is waiting for you to return home. Bring him the cherries you collected and live on as a happy family! <3

The game is playable, but still not finished. I am working on the design of levels 2 and 3 at the moment. They will be set in different locations and will implement some new mechanics such as usable doors, levers, spikes. There is still more to be done, but never fear because your favourite fox will be here!
